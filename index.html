About me

Hi! Thank you for visiting my website.

My name is Evie Meg and I am a self taught mandala artist. Ever since I was a little girl I have loved being creative. With painting, colouring, arts and crafts it's always been a hobby of mine. From drawing pictures for my teddy bears, to now having my own shop and selling my art work it's just amazing.

I've been drawing mandalas for around 7 years now. I love experimenting with paints, using bright colours and bringing more colour to people's homes. I am also a disabled artist, suffering with a condition in which my brain is inflamed. Sometimes I am unable to even hold a pen, which makes every art piece even more special. I hope with my art work and clothing I can bring a bit of colour and positivity to your day.

Lots of love,
Evie x
